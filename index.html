Frequently Asked Questions

After reading this page, it is understandable that some questions were raised. It's been 30 years of SINFO and there is always something that can be explained better. Here are some of the most Frequently Asked Questions from past editions. If your question is not listed, contact us in the next section.

What happened to the Diamond Package?

We decided to stop offering our Exclusive Package, also known as the Diamond Package. We felt that the format of the package is no longer in sync with our vision of the event.

How should I title my Presentation or Workshop?

The titles should not exceed 30 characters, and, at the same time, should be appealing and descriptive of the tematics discussed during the session. According to our statistics the companies who follow these guidelines end up having a higher crowded session compared to those who don't.

In which format should I send the logo of the company?

The logo should be sent in vectorial format (.ai, .svg, .eps, .pdf, etc.). If none of those is available, you should send us a .png with the maximum resolution available.
If the company has different logos for different color backgrounds, you should send us all of the possible options.
